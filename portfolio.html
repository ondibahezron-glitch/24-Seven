Sales Forecast Model for E-Commerce Retailer

Problem:

A growing e-commerce business struggled with inventory management due to inaccurate sales predictions. Manual forecasting led to frequent stockouts and overstocking, costing 20% in lost revenue.

Solution:

Built a time series forecasting model using ARIMA and seasonal decomposition. Integrated 3 years of historical sales data, accounting for trends, seasonality, and promotional effects.

Results:
  • 15% accuracy improvement over manual forecasts
  • $50K+ annual savings from optimized inventory
  • 30% reduction in stockouts during peak season
  • Automated weekly forecasts delivered via dashboard

Churn Prediction Model for SaaS Startup

Problem:

A subscription-based startup experienced 25% monthly churn but couldn't identify at-risk customers until after they cancelled. They needed proactive intervention strategies.

Solution:

Developed a logistic regression model to predict churn probability using usage patterns, support tickets, and billing data. Created a risk-scoring dashboard for the customer success team.

Results:
  • 82% prediction accuracy (AUC score: 0.85)
  • 18% churn reduction within 3 months
  • $120K+ revenue saved through retention campaigns
  • Top 3 churn drivers identified and addressed

Marketing A/B Test for FinTech Company

Problem:

A fintech company was unsure whether their new email campaign design truly improved conversions compared to the old design. They needed statistical evidence to guide future campaigns.

Solution:

Designed and executed a rigorous A/B test with 10,000 users. Performed hypothesis testing (two-sample t-test) and calculated confidence intervals for conversion rates.

Results:
  • 12% conversion rate increase (statistically significant, p < 0.01)
  • 95% confidence interval: [9.5%, 14.5%] improvement
  • $30K+ projected annual revenue from improved conversions
  • Test design template for future experiments

Data Automation Pipeline for Logistics Company

Problem:

A logistics company manually downloaded delivery reports from 5 platforms every Monday, spending 3 hours consolidating data. Process was error-prone and delayed weekly reviews.

Solution:

Built a Python automation script using APIs to pull data from all platforms, clean and merge it, then email a consolidated Excel report to stakeholders. Scheduled to run every Monday at 6 AM.

Results:
  • 3 hours/week saved (150+ hours/year)
  • Zero manual errors in data consolidation
  • Reports ready before work starts every Monday
  • Easily extensible to additional data sources
