Your data security is my top priority. Here's how I protect your information:

  • Encrypted transfers: All data is shared via secure, encrypted channels (HTTPS, SFTP)
  • Non-disclosure agreements: I sign NDAs before handling sensitive data
  • Local processing: Data is stored on my secure local machine, not cloud services
  • Data deletion: All project files are deleted after delivery unless you request otherwise
  • No third-party sharing: Your data is never shared with anyone without your explicit permission

I follow industry best practices for data security and comply with data protection regulations.

Timelines vary by project complexity:

  • Basic data cleaning & analysis: 3-5 days
  • Dashboard creation (Power BI/Tableau): 5-7 days
  • Statistical testing & A/B tests: 4-6 days
  • Predictive models: 7-10 days
  • Automation scripts: 5-7 days

Factors that affect timing:

  • Data quality (messy data takes longer to clean)
  • Scope of analysis (number of variables, models to test)
  • Revision rounds (typically 1-2 included in price)
  • Your responsiveness (answering clarifying questions)

Rush delivery (1-3 days) is available for an additional 50% fee for urgent projects.
